About

Your one stop for a complete range of Professional Home Improvement Services.

In case you aren't familiar with remodeling terminology, subcontractors are contractors with a specific area of expertise, such as plumbers, electricians, carpenters, and floor covering specialists. A general contractor has a broad knowledge of all aspects of construction and serves to coordinate the project, bringing in the subcontractors when appropriate during the work schedule. At QR Contracting Inc., we wear both hats. We act as a general contractor and as a subcontractor, as our company employs over 20 skilled craftsman in all major areas of construction. If needed QR Contracting will also bring in sub contractors for other specific jobs. We service commercial as well as residential areas. The fact that we provide both services is a major benefit to our customers saving a great deal of expense and time generally associated with bringing in various subcontractors to complete projects. At QR Contracting Inc., we understand the major frustration with contractors is that they tell you what you want to hear, then fail to perform. At QR, the fact that we are a general contractor and employee a full staff of craftsman means your job will always get done on schedule. Rather than waiting for sub-contractors to arrive to do various tasks which normally slows down the progress a great deal, (as phase two cannot begin till phase 1 is complete) our staff works hand in hand from one phase to the next without any down time in the process. Our bottom line goal is to satisfy our customers. We strive to do our work well and beyond the expectations of our customers. The staff at QR Contracting Inc. will focus on your needs and desires for your next project and can assist you right from the design/planning phase to finished construction, and all our work is guarantied to our customers satisfaction. Whether your in need of some handyman repair help - thinking of remodeling or finishing, expanding your house or even new construction, call us, we're always happy to give FREE estimates. We at QR Contracting Inc. are confident that you won't find better pricing for any profesional work that we provide. After you have gotten other quotes on your project, please call us for a free estimate.
